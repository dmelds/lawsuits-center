Ozempic and GLP-1 Medication Lawsuits

Ozempic and other GLP-1 medications have been the subject of litigation involving reports of serious gastrointestinal complications and other side effects.

See If You May Qualify

What Are Ozempic Lawsuits?

Some lawsuits claim that manufacturers failed to adequately warn patients and doctors about potential risks associated with GLP-1 medications.

These cases are part of broader active lawsuits involving pharmaceutical products and alleged side effects.

Who May Qualify

  • Individuals who used Ozempic or similar GLP-1 medications
  • Patients who experienced serious gastrointestinal complications
  • Those who required medical treatment related to side effects

Reported Complications

  • Severe gastrointestinal issues
  • Stomach paralysis (gastroparesis)
  • Persistent nausea or vomiting
  • Other digestive complications

Why People Are Exploring Claims

Individuals who experienced severe medical complications after using GLP-1 medications may be exploring whether they have legal options.
