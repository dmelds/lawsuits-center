Ozempic and GLP-1 Medication Lawsuits

Ozempic and other GLP-1 medications have been the subject of litigation involving reports of serious gastrointestinal complications and other alleged side effects.

What Are Ozempic Lawsuits?

These lawsuits generally involve allegations that manufacturers failed to adequately warn patients and doctors about potential risks associated with certain GLP-1 medications.

These cases are part of broader active lawsuits involving pharmaceutical products and alleged side effects.

Who May Qualify for Review

  • Individuals who used Ozempic or similar GLP-1 medications
  • Patients who experienced serious gastrointestinal complications
  • Those who required medical treatment related to side effects
  • Individuals trying to determine whether their experience fits a broader pattern seen in similar claims

Whether a person may qualify depends on the facts of the situation, including medication history, timing, reported complications, treatment required, and other case-specific details.

Reported Complications

  • Severe gastrointestinal issues
  • Stomach paralysis, often referred to as gastroparesis
  • Persistent nausea or vomiting
  • Other digestive complications
