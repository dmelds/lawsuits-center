Hair Relaxer Lawsuit

Hair relaxer lawsuits involve allegations that certain chemical hair straightening products may increase the risk of serious health conditions, including uterine cancer and other reproductive system disorders.

Multiple lawsuits have been filed against manufacturers of chemical hair relaxer products claiming that the companies failed to adequately warn consumers about potential health risks associated with long-term use.

Who May Qualify

  • Used chemical hair relaxer products regularly
  • Diagnosed with uterine cancer or other reproductive health conditions
  • Used products over an extended period of time

Current Litigation

Many hair relaxer lawsuits have been consolidated into federal multidistrict litigation (MDL) to coordinate pretrial proceedings. These cases continue to develop as courts evaluate scientific evidence and individual claims.

Legal Options

Individuals who believe they may have been affected by chemical hair relaxer products may wish to explore their legal options and determine whether they qualify to participate in ongoing litigation.
