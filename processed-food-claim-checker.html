What This Checks

Why this asks about diagnosis, not diet.

Almost everyone eats ultra-processed food, so consumption alone does not tell you anything about a claim. What the filed cases actually turn on is a serious metabolic diagnosis that began unusually early, paired with heavy, documentable consumption. This checker is built around that, so it screens on the diagnosis first and is honest when nothing fits.

  • The diagnosis, since the filed cases center on Type 2 diabetes and non-alcoholic fatty liver disease
  • Age at diagnosis, because early onset is what separates these claims from ordinary cases
  • Childhood and adolescent consumption history
  • A few exclusions that point to a different cause
  • State, because deadlines and rules vary
