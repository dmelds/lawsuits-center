David Meldofsky

Creator of Lawsuit Center · Licensed Attorney (California)

David Meldofsky is a licensed attorney in California and the creator of Lawsuit Center, a platform focused on helping individuals explore lawsuits, recognize potential legal situations, and understand how claims develop.

Background

David has worked within the legal referral and intake ecosystem, collaborating with law firms and supporting the intake, review, and referral of potential claims across multiple litigation areas.

This experience provides direct insight into how cases are screened, developed, and pursued, as well as where individuals often encounter confusion when trying to understand their options.

Approach

Lawsuit Center is designed to organize legal information around real-world situations — such as exposure, diagnosis, or product use — rather than abstract legal concepts.

The goal is to help individuals recognize patterns that may be relevant to their situation and better understand how different types of claims are typically structured.

Projects

  • Lawsuit Center — A platform for exploring lawsuits and identifying potential claims
  • Lawsuit Informer — Educational content explaining how lawsuits work and legal processes
