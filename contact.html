Tell Us About Your Situation

Many lawsuits begin with patterns — exposure over time, a diagnosis, or similar experiences reported by others. A short description is enough to start.






What Information Helps

  • What you were exposed to, used, or experienced
  • When it happened or how long it occurred
  • Any diagnosis or symptoms you received
  • Where the exposure or event took place

Even if you only know part of this information, you can still submit your message.

What Happens Next

Your message will be reviewed to better understand your situation and whether it may relate to broader legal patterns or existing claims.

Not every submission will result in follow-up, but each message helps determine whether additional steps may be appropriate.
