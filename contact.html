What to Include

What information helps.

  • What product, exposure, accident, diagnosis, medication, device, or topic is involved
  • When it happened or how long it occurred
  • Any symptoms, diagnosis, injuries, complications, or losses involved
  • Where the exposure, product use, accident, or event took place
  • Whether you are reaching out as an individual, family member, or law firm

Even if you only know part of this information, you can still submit a brief message.

After You Send

What happens next.

Messages may be reviewed to understand whether the inquiry relates to a possible case review, a general question, or a law firm placement discussion.

If the submission appears relevant to a lawsuit category or sponsored placement discussion, additional follow-up may be considered based on the information provided.

Not every submission will result in follow-up, and submission does not guarantee that you qualify for a claim, will be contacted by a law firm, will be offered representation, or will be offered placement.
