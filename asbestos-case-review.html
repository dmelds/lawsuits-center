Who this page may help

People researching mesothelioma, asbestos-related lung cancer, asbestosis, secondhand exposure, military exposure, shipyard exposure, refinery exposure, or other industrial asbestos histories.

What people often want to know

Whether a diagnosis, work history, time period, job site, or product exposure background may justify further review.

What happens here

You can submit basic information for a case review request so your situation can be screened for possible follow-up.

Situations people often research

  • Mesothelioma after past asbestos exposure
  • Lung cancer after known or suspected asbestos exposure
  • Asbestosis or serious respiratory disease tied to past work conditions
  • Exposure in shipyards, plants, refineries, industrial sites, construction, demolition, or military service
  • Secondhand asbestos exposure through a family member’s work clothes or equipment
  • Questions about old job sites, insulation, boilers, pumps, valves, gaskets, friction products, or other industrial materials
