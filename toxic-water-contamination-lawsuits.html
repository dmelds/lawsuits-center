Toxic Water Contamination Lawsuits

Water contamination lawsuits involve harmful chemicals entering drinking water supplies. These cases often arise when industrial chemicals, firefighting foam, or military base contamination exposes communities to toxic substances.

Common Causes of Water Contamination

  • PFAS chemicals ("forever chemicals")
  • Industrial chemical dumping
  • Military base contamination
  • Landfill runoff
  • Manufacturing waste

Health Problems Linked to Contaminated Water

  • Cancer
  • Kidney disease
  • Thyroid disorders
  • Immune system damage
  • Developmental problems
