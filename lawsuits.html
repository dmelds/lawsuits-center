AI Harm

AI chatbot harm, deepfakes, and image abuse.

A small but fast-growing category of lawsuits against AI companies. One group alleges that sustained chatbot use contributed to wrongful death by suicide, fatal drug interactions, acts of violence, or self-harm — pleaded mostly as strict products liability, negligence, and failure-to-warn rather than ordinary user-generated content. A second, newer group involves nonconsensual AI-generated images, deepfakes, and impersonation. The legal framework is developing and pending case outcomes are unpredictable.
